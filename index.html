Our Story

At Cake Bakes, every creation is a little spark of joy. We believe that a cake isn't just a dessert; it’s the centerpiece of your most cherished memories. We bake with love, imagination, and care, turning simple, high-quality ingredients into moments that make hearts glow. From our soft, luscious sponges to our rich, dreamy frostings, we put our heart and soul into every single detail.

Based in the heart of Sunwal, Nawalparasi, Nepal, we dedicate ourselves to making your celebrations sweeter and your smiles wider. We don't just follow recipes; we craft happiness, one batch at a time. Whether it’s a grand wedding or a small treat for yourself, our mission is to bring a touch of magic to your table with every bite.

Baking memories that last a lifetime, one bite at a time.
